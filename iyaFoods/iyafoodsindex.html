Our Founder - Toyin Kolawole

Growing up in Lagos Nigeria, food, family & enterpreneurship were always part of my life especially as the first daughter of 5 kids.Now as a mom of two, food is my love language here at home in lllinois.



Iya began in my kitchen where i really love making all sorts of fun nourishing foods with my boys using African ingredients. At iya,we create innovative, delicious, everyday foods made tastier and more nutritious with African superfoods.
